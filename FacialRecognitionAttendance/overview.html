CS 5331 Summer 2019

The aim of this project is to design and implement a classroom attendance recording system with a Raspberry Pi computer and camera module. The system utilizes a facial recognition model and training images of student faces to detect students as they walk into the classroom. A record with the class title, the date of lecture, students present during lecture, and the time students arrive class is automatically recorded. The list of students present for lecture is compared to a complete roster of the students enrolled in the course is used to create a record of students absent from the lecture.
